about me

I'm a senior CS student at KFUPM with a concentration in AI & ML. Outside of class, I actively pursue a fellowship by the Society of Actuaries (SOA) in actuarial science, which is basically math & statistics for quantifying risk and uncertainty. Although these may sound as different domains, machine learning and actuarial science are just probabilistic math at the end of the day. I've worked previously as a researcher at the Saudi Data and AI Authority (SDAIA) and as a backend engineer at Gaming+.

Got an idea? Pls get in touch — I would be happy to collaborate :)
